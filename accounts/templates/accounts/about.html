About the Cleaning Management System

The Cleaning Management System (CMS) at the University of Sri Jayewardenepura is a digital platform developed to modernize and streamline cleaning and maintenance operations within the campus. It connects administrators and management assistants through a single, efficient system that ensures transparency, accountability, and improved coordination.

By automating schedules, reports, and monitoring, the CMS reduces manual effort and improves the overall quality of maintenance services, helping to uphold the university’s commitment to a clean, sustainable, and well-managed environment.

Core Features

  • Automated creation and tracking of cleaning schedules.
  • Digital reporting and centralized record management.
  • Role-based dashboards for System Admins and Management Assistants.
  • Performance tracking and data analytics for maintenance efficiency.
  • Secure, user-friendly interface accessible through the university network.

System Objectives

  • Eliminate manual paperwork and reduce administrative workload.
  • Strengthen communication between administrative staff and assistants.
  • Ensure timely task completion and accurate performance tracking.
  • Promote sustainability through efficient resource management.

Our Vision

To build a clean, efficient, and digitally connected university environment that reflects the excellence of the University of Sri Jayewardenepura.

Our Mission

To simplify and enhance cleaning operations through a transparent, automated system that empowers staff and promotes continuous improvement in campus maintenance.
